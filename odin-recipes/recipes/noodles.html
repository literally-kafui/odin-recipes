Noodles Recipe

Description

Noodles is one of my favorites

I love it because it is so easy to prepare

It is also not time demanding

Ingredients

  • Indomie Onion Chicken
  • water
  • Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil the water for about 30 mins
  2. Place the indomie in the boiling water
  3. Allow to boil for 10 mins
  4. Pour the oil and add the vegetables
  5. Break the eggs into the oil and add the indomie
