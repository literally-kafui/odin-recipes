Rice Recipe

Description

Rice is a very delicious dish enjoyed by the poeple of Africa.

It is a cereal crop grown for its delicious taste.

Rice can be eaten with many kinds of stew.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water to about 100C
  2. Add a tablespoon of salt
  3. Add few drops of oil
  4. Wait for a few minutes and add the rice
  5. Leave to cook properly for about 45min
